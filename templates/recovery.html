System Recovery Module

The Recovery Module is designed to quickly restore system functionality in the event of abnormal behavior. If you encounter issues such as media audio stopping unexpectedly, USB devices not functioning properly, or the software not performing as expected, simply press the "Start" button below to initiate the recovery process. The module will then scan the system for any unusual changes, deadlocks, or other disruptions. If any issues are detected, it will attempt to automatically recover the system and software, ensuring minimal downtime and restoring normal operations without the need for a full restart or manual troubleshooting.
